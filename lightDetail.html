Light in the Darkness

During my Interactive Content Creation course, I created a short animatic based on my own poem that I wrote. This thought me a lot about creating individual frames for my animatic and about Adobe After Effects functions while editing my final video. I also did some audio work, adding sound effects and recording a voiceover and matching it to the animatic.

"Sometimes I feel like I'm standing inside a black void.
Nothing but darkness around me and even though I'm standing, I feel like there's no solid ground.
That if I take a step, I'll fall and just keep falling and falling...and falling.
But you are like a dot of light in that darkness. You make me want to take a risk and take a step.
So I do. I take a step and then another and soon I'm running towards the dot of light that is becoming bigger and bigger.
But isn't it always the light that makes you forget what hides in the darkness? Isn't it the light that blinds you?
I take another step, and the ground beneath me crumbles. And I fall. And I just keep falling and falling...and falling.
Will you catch me?"
