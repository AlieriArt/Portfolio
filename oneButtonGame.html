One Button Game ~ Fly Catcher

In my Creative Studio module, I had an assignment to come up with a concept for a game that relys on clicking only one button. I chose to make a cosy game called Fly Catcher. As you can see in the video example below, the frogs tongue can be charged by click and hold and releasing the button releases the tongue. The frog has mutiple levels of hungriness so the player has to catch the flies to keep it alive and earn coins. For adding extra interest to the game, I came up with different types of flies that each have a different bonus after being caught and also frog customisation where a player can use their collected coins to buy different frog colours, clothes or accesories for their frog.
